[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows are falling across the hardwood floor
I am stepping softly through your open door
Pull me closer, let the silence consume
Lost inside this endless dark lonely room

[Verse 1]
The fire is fading down to an ember's glow
There is nowhere else we ever need to go
I trace the lines of the twilight on your skin
This is the moment where our lives begin

[Pre-Chorus]
Every whisper feels so heavy in the air
I am tangled in the magic of your stare
The world outside is fading out of sight
We are the only ones alive tonight

[Chorus]
So let me hold you in the endless dark lonely room
Where every shadow is a flower in bloom
Your touch is a melody I cannot erase
I am completely lost in this beautiful space

[Post-Chorus]
Lost in the endless dark lonely room
Lost in the magic, lost in the bloom

[Verse 2]
The moonlight is painting the edge of the bed
I am holding onto every word that you said
Your breath on my shoulder is pulling me near
There is nowhere to be, but right now and right here

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every heartbeat is pulling us closer again
I am burning up like a desert in rain
The world outside is fading out of sight
We are the only ones alive tonight

[Chorus]
So let me hold you in the endless dark lonely room
Where every shadow is a flower in bloom
Your touch is a melody I cannot erase
I am completely lost in this beautiful space

[Post-Chorus 2]
Lost in the endless dark lonely room
Lost in the magic, lost in the bloom

[Bridge]
Lost inside this endless dark lonely room
Where every shadow is a flower in bloom
I am laying my soul out right here on the line
Saeed will be yours until the end of time
No other moment could ever compare
Just breathe with me now in the midnight air

[Spoken]
I never knew the dark could feel so bright
Just you and me, stopping time tonight

[Final Chorus]
So let me hold you in the endless dark lonely room
Where every shadow is a flower in bloom
Your touch is a melody I cannot erase
I am completely lost in this beautiful space
